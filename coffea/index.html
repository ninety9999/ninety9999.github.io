9+ Years of Craft
Our Story

Obsessed with the quiet details.

COFFEA began with a single roaster, a borrowed grinder, and a belief that great coffee doesn't need to shout. We source rare, ethically grown beans from small farms in Ethiopia, Colombia, and Guatemala — and roast them in-house, in small batches, every single morning.

Every drink is weighed, timed, and poured by hand. It's slower this way. It's also better. We think you'll taste the difference in the first sip.

— The COFFEA Team
